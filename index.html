STORYLINE

When the vicious Saxons began their campaign of raiding the eight wealthiest villages of Denmark, all but the hamlet of Englefield was turned to ruins. Within the burning agony and destruction, the villagers joined hands in a ritual of Lord Zeus that gave rise to the Eight Supreme Norsemen: The Vikings. Norsemen: Battle of Englefield follows the quest of the Vikings and how they saved the medieval world from the ruthless Saxons.
