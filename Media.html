Characters

Deacon

Deacon is the hawk-eyed archer and messenger of the Viking gods who was endowed with an indestructible golden vest to salvage the villagers of the Norsemen community from the wrath of Ares, the God of War. He is highly regarded as the “Shephard of the Men”

Dreng

Dreng, the assistant of the commander in chief, Harald, is quick at his feet which keeps the enemies guessing about his next move. The Viking gods were impressed by his power and witty decisions and gifted him the power of a hundred horses. He stops at nothing and does not take defeat for an answer.

Harald

Harald Fairhair, the first ever commander in chief of the Viking infantry troopers. The guerrilla leader lead them to conquer the land of the Irish kings. As mighty as a lion Harald never takes defeat for an answer.

Magnus

On the surface, Magnus has oodles of snark and dry sass. He shrugs things off, pokes at people and lets the chip fall where it may. But beneath that he is a deeply wise, cautious, and jaded man. He’s no longer waiting for a perfect happy ending because he knows it’s not there.

Raganr

Ragnar Lothbrok, the greatest hero of his age. He rises from the ground to become the king of the Viking tribe. A fearless warrior, Ragnar embodies the Norse traditions of devotion to the gods. Legend has it that he was a direct descendant of Odin, the god of war and warriors.

Skylar

The immortal Viking warrior, was capable of crossing the boundaries between the living and the dead and carrying the souls of the dead to Hades. The symbols of Skylar were the Talaria (winged sandals), the Caduceus (a short staff entwined by two snakes).

Ulf

Ulf is the avatar of the Viking god of thunder, Loki. This nocturnal savior of the Vikings community swiftly moves through the darkness to electrocute the evil. He carries an enchanted hammer of Mjolnir, one that can be lifted only by the worthy.

Taurus

Born with the power of a thousand bulls, Taurus has locked horns with the mightiest of the Gods to prove to them who stands their ground. The leader of the Viking community, a prophecy was made that his son would eventually overthrow him.

Torsten

With the power of Thor and the built of a rock, this Viking can hit enemies like a boulder. A precocious newborn, he invented the lyre and stole Apollo’s cattle on the very first day of his life.
